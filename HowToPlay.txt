HOW TO PLAY:
Player is given three balls
Kill all enemies

-Main Menu
 -Press start to play game

-Level 1
 -Drag ball to shoot towards the enemies to kill them
 -If player fails, auto retry
 -If player wins, option to go to main menu or next level

- Level 2
 -Drag ball to shoot towards the enemies to kill them
 -If player fails, auto retry
 -If player wins, YOU WIN or go to main menu
